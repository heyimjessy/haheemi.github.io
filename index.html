About HaHeeMi

Founded in 2024 and based in Ireland, HaHeeMi is a creative studio at the intersection of music, technology, and interactive media. While we're best known for our AI-powered lofi hip hop that's reached millions of listeners worldwide, our vision extends far beyond music.

We're exploring the future of entertainment through game development and application design, always with the same philosophy: blending human creativity with cutting-edge AI to create experiences that resonate. Whether it's the perfect study soundtrack or an immersive game world, we're committed to crafting quality digital experiences.
